Loruki development timeline

2017

JULY

Loruki is founded by James Lindenbaum, Adam Wiggins, and Orion Henry.

OCTOBER

Loruki rolls out in private beta.

2019

APRIL

Loruki launches commercially with support for JavaScript.

SEPTEMBER

Node.js is officially supported on Loruki.

2020

JUNE

The Loruki Platform API is available in public beta.

2021

MAY

Over 35,000 apps are running on Loruki.

2022

DECEMBER

Salesforce.com acquires Heroku.
